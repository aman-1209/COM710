Solent-Activities

Activities Introduction

With more than thirty sports clubs that compete in regional and national events, such as the British Universities and Colleges Sport (BUCS) championships, Solent University provides plenty of activities to do. The annual Varsity game, a passionate matchup with Bournemouth University that unites the student body, is the highlight of the athletic calendar. Apart from offering competitive sports, Solent also enhances professional preparedness by offering real-world student placements in sports administration and coaching. Notable groups include Team Solent Basketball, which practices at Sports Centre St Mary's, and Team Solent Football, which plays at the Test Park sports field.

SPORTS CLUBS

There are over 30 sports clubs at Solent, so you're bound to find one you want to join! Many compete in local and national competitions, as well as the BUCS championships.

VARSITY

The highlight of our sporting year - Varsity is an annual event between Solent University and Bournemouth University that takes place over two days.

STUDENT PLACEMENTS

Solent Sport student placements offers you the opportunity to get the most out of your student experience.

TEAM SOLENT FOOTBALL

Team Solent Football is one of the most successful university football clubs in recent years. Both the men's and women's teams play their home games at Solent's Test Park sports ground.

TEAM SOLENT BASKETBALL

Team Solent Kestrels has a men's and women's squad. Both teams train at Sports Centre St Mary's and carry on a long tradition of basketball at Solent University.

TEAM SOLENT CRICKET

Team Solent Cricket competes in the top university league, regularly playing against some of the best teams in the country including Loughborough, Durham and Oxford.

TEAM SOLENT WHEELCHAIR RUGBY

Team Solent Sharks competes in the Great Britain Wheelchair Rugby (GBWR) Super Series league and plays a key role in promoting the sport for disabled athletes.
