About Us

Solent History

Solent has been a university since July 2005, but it has a long and complex educational history. Incorporated as an independent higher education institution in 1989, origins can be traced back to a private School of Art founded in 1855. For more

Solent Ambition

We aim to empower students by increasing their confidence and improving their career opportunities. We are committed to constantly improving our educational programs to make sure every student can reach their highest potential. For more

Solent Gym

The gym is fully staffed at all times by our professional trainers and instructors. It is open to all Solent students and staff, as well as the general public. There are no joining fees and plenty of memberships available to suit you. For more

Welcome

Solent University is all about creating opportunities: for its students, for its staff, for its partners. It offers expertise and experiences you couldn’t get anywhere else, opening doors and making introductions.

We’re focused on the practical side of higher education – giving our students the connections, experience and confidence to turn their newfound knowledge into a career with real, meaningful impact. Whoever you are, whatever your goals, we want to help you make your mark.
